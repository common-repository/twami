Twami displays a beautiful e-mail subscriber box in the bottom of your website.

== Description ==

With Twami you can easily:
- Customize the design
- Decide when it should appear
- Implement it on your website
- Integrate it with your mailing list software

Our first split tests boost e-mail subscriptions by 400% - 1.000% without decreasing the sales conversion rate.

To use this Twami Wordpress plugin you need to have a Twami account and a Twami customer ID. You can signup for a Twami account here: www.twami.com 

== Installation ==

1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress

== Frequently asked questions ==

What is so great about Twami? 
The customers who tried Twami have typically increased 
their email subscribers by 400%

Is Twami annoying like other signup popup systems? 
Unlike other email subscriber services Twami won’t interrupt visitors. The box displays gently and can easily be closed.

Can I customize Twami to my own website design? 
You can completely customize the content and the look. You can choose from a ton of colours, change text and even upload your own images to Twami.

Does Twami work on all platforms? 
Yes, it works on all website platforms. Simply insert a small piece of code and you’re good to go! It only takes 2 minutes.

The Twami graphics run on our servers so you don't need any advanced options or settings in your platform.

What about my subscribers - where will they be saved? 
Subscribers get into your mailing list software. We support the most popular mailing list software including: MailChimp, Campaign Monitor, AWeber etc.
